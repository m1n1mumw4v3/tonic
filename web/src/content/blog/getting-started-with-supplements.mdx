Starting a supplement routine can feel overwhelming. With thousands of options on the market, how do you know what's right for you? Here's a practical framework.

## Start with your goals

Before reaching for any bottle, ask yourself: what am I trying to improve? Better sleep? More energy? Sharper focus? Your goals should drive your choices — not trends or marketing.

## The foundation stack

Most people benefit from a few evidence-based basics:

- **Vitamin D** — especially if you spend most of your day indoors
- **Magnesium** — supports sleep, mood, and muscle recovery
- **Omega-3s** — foundational for heart and brain health

## Build gradually

Don't start five supplements at once. Add one at a time, wait a week or two, and notice how you feel. This makes it much easier to identify what's actually working.

## Track how you feel

The most underrated habit in supplementation is simply paying attention. A daily check-in — even just a quick rating of your sleep, energy, and mood — creates the feedback loop that makes your routine smarter over time.
