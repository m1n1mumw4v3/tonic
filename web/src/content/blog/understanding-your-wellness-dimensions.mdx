Health isn't a single number. It's a spectrum of interconnected dimensions that shift from day to day. That's why Estus tracks five.

## Sleep

How you rest determines how you recover. Quality sleep is the foundation of every other dimension — when sleep improves, energy, mood, and clarity tend to follow.

## Energy

Not just "are you tired?" but how sustained and stable your energy feels throughout the day. Spikes and crashes matter as much as the average.

## Clarity

Mental sharpness, focus, and the ability to think clearly. This is often the first dimension to respond to changes in supplementation.

## Mood

Emotional balance and resilience. Not about being happy all the time — but about feeling steady and capable of handling what comes your way.

## Gut

Digestion and comfort. The gut-brain connection is one of the most active areas of health research, and it plays a larger role in overall wellness than most people realize.

## The whole picture

No single dimension tells the whole story. By tracking all five, you start to see patterns — like how poor sleep on Monday ripples into low clarity by Wednesday, or how a new supplement steadily lifts your energy over two weeks.

That's the power of dimensional wellness tracking.
