# Core Onboarding Survey Questions

## 1. Name
**What's your first name?**
- **Type:** Text input (free entry)

---

## 2. Date of Birth
**What's your date of birth?**
- **Type:** Date picker (wheel), range: age 18-100

---

## 3. Biological Sex
**What's your biological sex?**
- Male
- Female
- Non-binary
- Prefer not to say

---

## 4. Pregnancy Status
**Are you currently pregnant or breastfeeding?**
**Condition: Female only**
- No
- Pregnant
- Breastfeeding

*Note: Pregnant and Breastfeeding can both be selected simultaneously.*

---

## 5. Height
**How tall are you?**
- **Type:** Scroll wheel picker with unit toggle (ft/in or cm)
- **Range:** 3'0" - 7'11" (91 - 241 cm)

---

## 6. Weight
**What's your current weight?**
- **Type:** Scroll wheel picker with unit toggle (lbs or kg)
- **Range:** 60 - 500 lbs (27 - 227 kg)

---

## 7. Health Goals
**What are your top health goals?**
**Type: Multi-select (up to 4)**
- More energy
- Better sleep
- Stress & anxiety relief
- Mental clarity & focus
- Gut health & digestion
- Immune support
- Muscle growth & recovery
- Skin, hair & nails
- Heart health
- Longevity

---

## 8. Diet
**What best describes how you eat?**
- No specific diet (Omnivore)
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Keto
- Paleo
- Pescatarian
- Halal
- Mediterranean
- Low-Carb
- Other (free text entry, e.g. "Whole30, Carnivore...")

---

## 9. Exercise Frequency
**How often do you exercise?**
- None
- 1-2x / week
- 3-4x / week
- 5+ / week

---

## 10. Allergies
**Any known allergies or sensitivities?**
**Type: Multi-select + free text**
- No known allergies
- Shellfish
- Soy
- Gluten
- Dairy
- Tree Nuts
- Fish
- Eggs
- Peanuts
- Other allergies (free text entry, e.g. "Sesame, Latex...")

*Note: Selecting "No known allergies" clears all other selections.*

---

## 11. Current Supplements
**Are you currently taking any supplements?**
- Yes / No toggle
- If Yes: opens a searchable supplement picker sheet + custom text entry
- Displayed as removable chips

---

## 12. Medications
**Are you currently taking any medications?**
- Yes / No toggle
- If Yes: opens a searchable medication picker sheet + custom text entry
- Displayed as removable chips

---

## 13. Caffeine Intake
**How much caffeine do you drink daily?**
**Type: Stepper controls per category**
- None (resets all to 0)
- Coffee — ___ Cups
- Tea — ___ Cups
- Energy Drink — ___ Cans
- Soda — ___ Cans

- None

- 8+ drinks / week

## 15. Stress Level

- None

## 16. Wellness Baseline
**Rate your typical wellness levels**
*"Be honest — this helps establish a true baseline."*
**Type: Slider (0-10) for each dimension**

| Dimension | Low Label (0) | High Label (10) |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Sleep | Restless / Broken | Deep & Restorative |
| Energy | Drained / Fatigued | Vibrant & Sustained |
| Clarity | Foggy / Scattered | Sharp & Locked In |
| Mood | Low / Flat | Positive & Balanced |
| Gut | Uncomfortable / Off | Smooth & Settled |
